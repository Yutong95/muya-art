MUYA (Make Unique, YoungArts) shows the cultural charm of independent art and the vitality of Young creation. Young people nowadays, we get out of our comfort zone and break down dimensional walls to create many new ideas based on basic knowledge. We sincerely invite those who work in contemporary art, design, illustration, craft, street art, animation, new materials, new media, curators, studio partners, creators, film and television, writing, music, and other outstanding new stars and predecessors to do creative dialogues.

Beiya Yang examines common objects. She likes to observe life, and she has brought this habit into her works. After finishing, an undergraduate degree in Craft and materials study at Virginia Commonwealth University. She went to the State University of New York -New Platz to continue her metal research project.

More recently, she has focused on specifically interpreting the language of ubiquitous clothing labels. By extracting their contents, she creates new labels to express personal reflections on everyday routines and rituals. As jewelry, the tagsâ€™ inner language is exposed, inviting the wearer to consider their own contents, habits, and needs. Her works are selected in exhibition at Beijing International Design Week, New York Jewelry Week, Madrid Art Jewelry Week in Spain, Munich Jewelry Week in France, French Jewelry Week, Athens Jewelry Week, German Jewelry Week, Anderson Gallery, Philadelphia National Liberty Museum, etc. Beiya Yang is currently completing her graduate degree in metal.
